Welcome to the Particle Simulator

This application is a physics-based particle simulator designed to demonstrate fundamental physics principles in an interactive environment.

Features:

  • Interactive Controls: Customize particle shapes, count, and behavior.
  • Real-Time Physics: Observe particles interacting under forces like gravity and collision.
  • Dynamic Visualization: Change particle colors and interaction modes for varied simulations.

Use the controls to modify the simulation parameters and watch how particles respond to different physical forces. Click anywhere on the canvas to apply additional forces and influence particle movement.
